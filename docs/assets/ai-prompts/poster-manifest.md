WWII-era military operations room, radar scope glow, amber instrument panel lighting, dark navy background, Courier-type labels on instrument dials, technical illustration style, flat graphic poster aesthetic, no photorealism, high contrast amber on black, signals intelligence aesthetic, 1940s wartime precision, screenprint color separation.
